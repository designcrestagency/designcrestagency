Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and templates.

What file formats do you provide?

All our templates come in both Photoshop (.psd) and Illustrator (.ai) formats, along with a PDF guide.

Do you offer custom design services?

Yes! We specialize in custom logo design, branding, UI/UX, and print media. Just fill out our Custom Order form.

How do I download my purchase?

After payment, you'll receive a download link via email. You can also access your files from your account dashboard.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 14-day refund policy for all digital products. Contact us within 14 days of purchase for a full refund.

Can I use your templates for commercial projects?

Yes, all our templates come with a commercial license. You can use them for client projects and commercial work.

Refund Policy

Our commitment to your satisfaction.

14-Day Refund Guarantee

We offer a 14-day full refund policy for all digital products purchased directly from our website.

How to Request a Refund

Send us an email with your order details and the reason for your refund request. Processed within 3-5 business days.

Exceptions

Custom design orders are non-refundable once work has begun. However, we offer unlimited revisions.

File Licenses

Understand how you can use our templates.

Standard License

All templates come with a standard commercial license. You may use them for personal and commercial projects.

What You CAN Do

  • Use templates for client work
  • Print for commercial use
  • Modify and customize as needed

What You CANNOT Do

  • Resell templates as your own

File Requirements

What you need to use our templates.

Software Requirements

  • Adobe Photoshop (CS6 or later)
  • Adobe Illustrator (CS6 or later)
  • Adobe InDesign (for some templates)

System Requirements

  • Windows 10+ or macOS 10.14+
  • Minimum 4GB RAM (8GB recommended)
  • 1GB free hard disk space

Fonts

All templates use free fonts or include font links. We provide a complete font list with every purchase.
